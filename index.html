Golf is a precision sport where players use clubs to hit a small ball into a series of holes on a course, aiming to do so in as few strokes as possible. Played on vast outdoor courses featuring varied terrain, golf requires skill, strategy, and patience. It is enjoyed both recreationally and professionally around the world, with major tournaments like The Masters and The Open drawing global attention.
